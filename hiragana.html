Learn Hiragana

The Essential Guide to Japanese Phonetic Script

What is Hiragana?

Hiragana is one of the three primary scripts in Japanese. It's a phonetic alphabet used for native Japanese words, grammatical functions, and pronunciation. Learning Hiragana is your first step in mastering the Japanese language!

Why Learn Hiragana?

  • It is the foundation of Japanese reading and writing.
  • It helps with reading native Japanese materials.
  • Enables deeper understanding of Kanji and Katakana.
  • Improves pronunciation and overall fluency.
